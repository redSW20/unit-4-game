Crystal Hoarder!

Here is what you do to steal the dragon's treasure:

  • You are to be given a random number at the beginning of the game that you need to match your score with.
  • Click on one of the four crystals below in order to increase your score at random.
  • When you match your score to the number given, your crytals transform into a win against the dragon.
  • If you lose then the dragon will have you for a snack!
